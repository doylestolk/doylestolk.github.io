About me

During my internship as a Physical Therapy student, I noticed that the feeling was not there. After some soul searching and many conversations later the decision was made to switch careers. I started out with an IT Traineeship where I could start at entry level. The emphasis was mostly on Microsoft services as that was part of their core business. The deal was to start there and gradually develop my skills to become a Software Developer (pun intended). As time passed my ambition could not be met by the traineeship. I had to move on to the next step and get closer towards achieving my goal. So, this year I started the accelerated course Java Back-end Developer at LOI University of Applied Science, and I am really enjoying it so far.

The course includes: PHP, MySQL, CSS, Javascript, HTML, Java and UML.
